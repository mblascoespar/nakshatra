# Nakshatra ↔ Tara Mapping Guide

## Understanding the Difference

### **Nakshatra** (The Day's Constellation)
- What you see: **"Day's Nakshatra"** at the top of each day's detail
- 27 fixed lunar mansions (Ashwini, Bharani, Krittika, etc.)
- Each has specific **auspicious activities** listed in the app
- These are from your documentation

**Example:** "Rohini nakshatra is good for laying foundations, buying real estate, marriage, etc."

---

### **Tara** (The Relationship to Your Birth)
- What you see: **"Tara Rating"** in the colored badge
- 9 relative positions between your birth nakshatra and the day's nakshatra
- Calculated as: `(day_nakshatra - your_birth_nakshatra) mod 9 + 1`

**Example:** If you're born in Ashwini (1) and today is Rohini (4):
- Tara = (4 - 1) mod 9 + 1 = **Tara 4 (Kshema)** = "Good"

---

## How to Map When Reading Documentation

1. **Open a day in the calendar**
2. **See "Day's Nakshatra"** at the top → (e.g., "Rohini")
3. **Check your document** for "Rohini" → Look up its activities
4. **See "Tara Rating"** → Tells you how favorable that day is *for you specifically*
   - Very Good (Tara 2, 6, 9) → Best days
   - Good (Tara 4, 8) → Good days
   - Mixed (Tara 1) → Neutral
   - Poor (Tara 3, 5) → Avoid if possible
   - Very Bad (Tara 7) → Strongly avoid

## Example Workflow

**You want to start a business on March 15th:**

1. ✅ Open the calendar → Click March 15
2. ✅ See **"Day's Nakshatra: Pushya"**
3. ✅ Check your document → Pushya is good for "Starting a business or industry"
4. ✅ See **"Tara Rating: Tara 2 (Sampat) - Very Good"** 
5. ✅ **Decision:** Perfect! Both the nakshatra activities AND the tara rating are favorable

## If You Want to Cross-Reference Manually

**For any day shown in the calendar:**

1. The **sunrise_nakshatra_name** in the calendar app
2. Look it up in your reference document under that nakshatra's name
3. Cross-check against the **tara tier** shown (Very Good/Good/Mixed/Poor/Very Bad)

**The app now displays both clearly:**
- **"Day's Nakshatra"** = Which constellation activities to check
- **"Tara Rating"** = How favorable that day is for you personally
